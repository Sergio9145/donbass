In accordance with the Ukrainian law on fighting the terrorism Ukrainian authorities officially announced the start of "anti-terrorist operation" also known as ATO against pro-Russian separatists.

An armoured column sent by Ukraine established a checkpoint 40 km from Sloviansk. The SBU claimed that the rebels there had been reinforced by several hundred soldiers from Russia's Main Intelligence Directorate. Ukrainian special forces retook Kramatorsk airfield from pro-Russian troops.
